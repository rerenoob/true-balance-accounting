What We Do

Comprehensive Accounting & Tax Services

Whether you're filing a personal return or need a full accounting department, we have a solution built for your situation.

Tax Preparation & Filing

Individual and business tax returns prepared accurately and filed on time. We identify every deduction you're entitled to — so you keep more of what you earn.

  • Individual & business returns (1040, 1120S, 1065)
  • IRS & state compliance
  • Prior year filing & amendments
  • Tax planning & strategy

Bookkeeping

Clean, organized books every single month. We handle the data entry, reconciliations, and reporting — so you always know exactly where your business stands financially.

  • Monthly bookkeeping & reconciliation
  • Profit & loss, balance sheet reports
  • QuickBooks Online & Xero support
  • Cash flow monitoring

Payroll

Your employees get paid correctly and on time, every time. We handle payroll processing, tax deposits, W-2s, and keep you fully compliant with federal and Texas payroll laws.

  • Weekly, bi-weekly & monthly payroll
  • Payroll tax filing & deposits
  • W-2 & 1099 preparation
  • New hire reporting

Outsourced Accounting

Get a full-service accounting department without the overhead of hiring in-house staff. We become your financial backbone — handling everything from AP/AR to month-end close.

  • Accounts payable & receivable

  • Financial reporting & analysis
  • CFO-level advisory support
