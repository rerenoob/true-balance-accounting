Client-Facing Project Ideas

Tools and features that directly benefit our clients and drive business growth

📋 For Business Partner Review

Purpose: This page highlights 9 client-facing projects that directly impact client experience, retention, and revenue growth. These are the projects clients will see and use.

🎯 Focus Areas:

  • Client Experience: Making it easier for clients to work with us
  • Retention: Keeping clients longer through better service
  • Upselling: Moving clients to higher service tiers
  • Efficiency: Reducing manual work for our team

⚡ Immediate Impact (Quarter 1-2)

Projects that deliver quick wins and establish our modern brand identity.

1. Smart Onboarding Form

HIGH PRIORITY

Interactive form that captures business details and automatically recommends the right service package.

Business Impact:
• Converts website visitors into qualified leads 24/7
• Reduces initial consultation time by 50%
• Professional first impression establishes trust
⏱️ Timeline: 2-3 weeks development
Benefits for Clients:
  • Quick, guided setup process
  • Immediate service recommendation
  • No back-and-forth emails

2. Client Portal (Document Vault)

HIGH PRIORITY

Secure online portal where clients upload documents, track progress, and download completed work.

Business Impact:
• Eliminates email document chaos
• Differentiates us from competitors using email
• Justifies higher pricing tiers
⏱️ Timeline: 4-6 weeks development
Pricing Tier Impact:
Starter Business Growth

All tiers get access — establishes modern brand

3. Appointment Booking System

MEDIUM PRIORITY

Self-service calendar for consultations, tax planning, and monthly review meetings.

Business Impact:
• Captures leads 24/7 (no phone tag)
• Reduces administrative scheduling work
• Professional appearance vs. email scheduling
⏱️ Timeline: 1-2 weeks (using existing tools)
Integration: Syncs with team calendars, sends automatic reminders, includes video conferencing links.

💰 Revenue Growth (Quarter 2-3)

Projects that increase client value and justify premium pricing.

4. Client Financial Dashboard

HIGH PRIORITY

Interactive dashboard showing P&L, cash flow, expenses with plain-English AI summaries.

Business Impact:
• Justifies $999/mo Growth tier pricing
• Massive retention driver (clients understand their finances)
• Competitive advantage vs. traditional firms
⏱️ Timeline: 6-8 weeks development
Pricing Tier Impact:
Starter Business Growth

Growth tier exclusive — key upsell feature

5. Automated Receipt Processing

MEDIUM PRIORITY

Clients take photos of receipts → AI extracts data → auto-categorizes in QuickBooks.

Business Impact:
• Eliminates most tedious bookkeeping task
• Enables real-time bookkeeping vs. month-end scramble
• Direct differentiator from competitors
⏱️ Timeline: 4-5 weeks development
Client Benefits:
  • No more shoebox of receipts
  • Instant expense tracking
  • Mobile app convenience

6. Monthly Financial Report Generator

MEDIUM PRIORITY

Automated monthly reports: pulls QuickBooks data → AI writes summary → emails PDF to client.

Business Impact:
• Delivers promised "Monthly Report Generator"
• Clients see ongoing value (not just tax season)
• Reduces manual report writing time
⏱️ Timeline: 3-4 weeks development
Included in: Business Growth

Automated, consistent, professional

🏆 Competitive Advantage (Quarter 3-4)

Projects that set us apart from traditional accounting firms.

7. Tax Q&A Assistant

MEDIUM PRIORITY

AI chatbot trained on IRS rules and Texas tax laws that answers client questions instantly.

Business Impact:
• Most local firms have nothing like this
• Handles common questions without staff time
• Potential standalone product later
⏱️ Timeline: 8-10 weeks development
Unique Selling Point: "Get instant answers to tax questions, 24/7, with citations from official sources."

8. Referral Program Portal

LOW PRIORITY

Clients get unique referral links → earn billing credits when referrals sign up.

Business Impact:
• Accounting referrals convert at 80%+ rates
• Word-of-mouth is #1 source for CPA clients
• Low-cost customer acquisition
⏱️ Timeline: 2-3 weeks development
Mechanics: $50 credit per successful referral, tracked automatically, visible in client portal.

9. Mobile Expense Tracker App

LOW PRIORITY

Simple mobile app for receipt capture, mileage tracking, and expense categorization.

Business Impact:
• "Sticky" product (clients use daily)
• Data flows automatically to our systems
• Modern, tech-forward brand image
⏱️ Timeline: 10-12 weeks development
Platforms: iOS & Android, syncs with client portal, offline capability.

🤝 Discussion Points for Partners

Budget & Resources

  • Development cost: $5K-15K per project (varies by complexity)
  • Timeline: 2-3 projects per quarter realistic
  • Team: Technical lead (Duong) + potential freelance help
  • Hosting: $50-100/month for infrastructure

Client Impact Assessment

  • Which clients would benefit most from each feature?
  • How do these align with our target client profile?
  • Which features help retain vs. acquire clients?
  • What pricing adjustments might these justify?

Risk & Considerations

  • Technical debt: Need to maintain what we build
  • Client training: Some clients may need help adopting new tools
  • Data security: Extra important for financial data
  • Scalability: Systems must grow with client count
